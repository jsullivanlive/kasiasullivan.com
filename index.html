Introduction

My goal and passion is to guide people to be healthy and confident through fitness: Teach body mechanics to train efficiently and in a way people enjoy. The human body has unlimited power to heal itself; give it the opportunity and environment to do so and you will notice an incredible difference in all aspects of your life. I have worked with clients in Cambridge, Somerville, and Arlington since 2005 and have led fitness classes at gyms throughout Cambridge.

I am certified, insured, and always valid and updated with safety programs such as CPR. What I offer in my services is a personal training session in your home which starts with a complementary consultation (we talk about your fitness goals and what you try to archive), fitness assessment (checking your weight, body fat, measurements), and postural assessments, so we can see progress in your training. I can bring to your home equipment like bosu ball, swiss ball, gliding disks, kettle belts, TRX, and kickboxing equipment. Most importantly, every training session is customized to your personal needs and goals.
